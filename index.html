Hog Gamers was established in January 2023 with a long-term vision of building a powerful and trusted esports brand in India. From the beginning, our focus has never been just on organizing matches ‚Äî our focus has always been on creating a competitive ecosystem where players are respected, supported, and rewarded fairly.


We understand that many players struggle to find tournaments that are transparent, affordable, and genuinely player-oriented. That is exactly the gap Hog Gamers was created to fill.


Our foundation stands on five strong pillars:

• Prize Guarantee ‚Äì Every committed reward is honored.

• Low Entry Fees ‚Äì Competitive gaming should be accessible.

• Low Margin Policy ‚Äì Players‚Äô growth is prioritized over excessive profit.

• Complete Transparency ‚Äì Clear rules, clear results, clear communication.

• Professional & Polite Management ‚Äì Respect for every player and team.


We are a Pan-India esports community aiming to nurture talent, promote fair competition, and gradually build a long-term esports brand that players can trust without hesitation.


Hog Gamers is not just organizing tournaments ‚Äî we are building a competitive culture.


Hog Gamers currently organizes competitive tournaments for Free Fire Max, with structured plans to expand into major esports titles such as BGMI, Indus, and Valorant in the upcoming phases.


We offer multiple competitive formats including:

• Full Map Solo

• Full Map Solo

• Full Map Duo

• Full Map Squad

• Clash Squad

• Paid Custom Matches

• Special Event & Invitational Tournaments


Our tournament roadmap includes daily, weekly, and seasonal events. While we are in our early growth stage, the long-term structure is designed to provide continuous competition opportunities throughout the year.


Prize pools vary depending on tournament type and mode. We conduct both entry-based and special reward tournaments, with payout systems including direct cash rewards, UPI transfers, and structured prize distributions.


Qualification structures differ based on the mode and tournament design to ensure competitive balance and fair opportunities for all participants.


Every tournament conducted under Hog Gamers is built on clarity, discipline, fair play, and structured coordination.
